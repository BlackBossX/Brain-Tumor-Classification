Assignment: Neural Network Classification of Brain Tumor Type
You are given a tabular medical dataset of 9,000 patient cases. Each record has 27 features, a mix
of numeric and categorical variables together with a target label, tumor_type, which takes one of
three values: Glioma, Meningioma, or Pituitary. The data is imperfect: it contains missing values
recorded under several inconsistent markers, categorical fields requiring encoding, features on
very different scales, class imbalance, and some features are uninformative.
Your task is to design, train, tune, and critically evaluate a feed-forward neural network (multilayer
perceptron) that classifies a case into one of the three tumour types. You are responsible for the
entire workflow, from cleaning and preprocessing the raw dataset to selecting and justifying one
final model.
Submit your final trained model so it can be tested on a separate held out dataset by the examiner.
